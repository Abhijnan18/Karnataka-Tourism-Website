Sri Manjunatha Swamy Temple, Dharmasthala

The Sri Manjunatha Swamy Temple in Dharmasthala, Karnataka, venerates Lord Shiva. Known for its interfaith harmony, it embraces devotees of all backgrounds. The temple's distinctive tradition offers free meals (annadanam), emphasizing altruism and unity, fostering a spiritual haven for seekers and a hub of selfless service.

Sri Kukke Subramanya Temple

Nestled in Subramanya village, Karnataka, the Sri Kukke Subramanya Temple is a revered abode of Lord Subramanya, the serpent deity. Pilgrims seek blessings for protection from serpentine troubles and obstacles. Enveloped by the Western Ghats' serene beauty, the temple holds an aura of spiritual solace, drawing devotees to its sanctified realm.

Shravanabelagola

Shravanabelagola, Karnataka, is renowned for its colossal monolithic statue of Lord Gomateshwara, an emblem of Jainism's ascetic values. Perched atop a hill, the statue radiates serenity and enlightenment. A vital Jain pilgrimage site, it offers panoramic vistas and evokes contemplation, epitomizing devotion and humility in its tranquil ambiance.

Sri Chamundeshwari Temple

Adorning Mysore's Chamundi Hill, the Sri Chamundeshwari Temple reveres the formidable Goddess Chamundeshwari. Her protective and divine energy blesses the city. The temple blends devotion with captivating vistas, offering a spiritual retreat. Its cultural significance and architectural beauty harmonize, creating a serene haven for devotees and visitors alike.
